C: Hybrid (Recommended)

Best of both: progress bar stepper + role-aware action banner + simplified line table + collapsed secondary sections with badge counts. Maximum signal, minimum noise. Everything on one screen, nothing hidden that matters.

Why C (Hybrid) Works Best

  • Progress bar instantly shows where the deal stands
  • Action banner tells each role exactly what to do next
  • Line items reduced from 10 to 6 columns with inline action links
  • AI flags, notes, SO verification collapsed but badge counts visible
  • Stats compressed to header row — no 6-card grid eating vertical space

Trade-offs

  • More complex template logic (role detection for action banner)
  • Collapsed sections require extra clicks to see details
  • Progress bar needs mapping for edge cases (halted, cancelled)
