# Sourcing Engine Smoke Test

1. Open a part requiring sourcing.
2. Trigger or view a sourcing run.
3. Verify leads appear with:
   - vendor name
   - confidence band
   - safety band
   - reason summary
   - source badges
4. Open a lead detail panel.
5. Verify:
   - evidence list is visible
   - source references are visible
   - safety summary is visible
   - contact information is visible
6. Mark a lead Contacted.
7. Add a note.
8. Change a lead to Has Stock or No Stock.
9. Confirm the lead appears in the correct queue/status view.
10. Verify the activity history reflects the actions.
11. Check at least one duplicate scenario and confirm leads are merged or flagged conservatively.
12. Check at least one new vendor and confirm safety review is shown with caution wording.
