# Beginner-Friendly Human Testing Checklist

## What to click
- Open a part
- Look at sourcing results
- Open a lead
- Check the reason summary
- Check the safety summary
- Use a quick status action
- Add a note
- Switch to follow-up queue

## What should happen
- Leads should be understandable at a glance
- Confidence and safety should both be visible
- Reason summary should make sense
- Source badges should match visible evidence
- Quick actions should update status immediately
- Notes should be saved and visible in history

## Bad results to watch for
- Duplicate vendors shown as separate leads
- Safety and confidence blended into one unclear score
- No way to tell why a lead exists
- No source references
- No contact path
- Status change not reflected in queue/history
- Scary or accusatory safety language
