# Phase Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Discovery
Map the current sourcing codebase and identify reusable models, routes, jobs, and UI components.

## Phase 2 — Schema foundation
Implement or extend one lead per vendor per part plus many evidence items.

## Phase 3 — Scoring separation
Create separate lead-confidence and vendor-safety calculations and store visible summaries.

## Phase 4 — Deduplication
Normalize vendor identity and merge only on strong signals.

## Phase 5 — Buyer workflow
Add statuses, quick actions, notes, and history.

## Phase 6 — Feedback loop
Capture buyer outcomes and use simple rule-based adjustments to future ranking.

## Phase 7 — UI
Build or extend sourcing results, detail panel, queue, and safety review.

## Phase 8 — Connector hardening
Improve freshness, attribution, contact enrichment, and graceful failure behavior.

## Phase 9 — QA / cleanup
Targeted testing, dead-code cleanup, edge-case hardening, and human testing prep.
