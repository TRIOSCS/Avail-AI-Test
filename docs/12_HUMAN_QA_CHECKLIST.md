# Human QA Checklist

## Core sourcing checks
- open sourcing screen for a part
- confirm results load
- confirm each lead clearly shows vendor, reason, confidence, safety, and source info
- confirm confidence and safety are visually separate
- confirm contact preview is visible
- confirm suggested next action is visible

## Detail view checks
- open a lead detail view
- confirm evidence is grouped and readable
- confirm source attribution is present
- confirm safety review is present
- confirm contact details are easy to find
- confirm buyer actions are present

## Workflow checks
- update a lead to Contacted
- update a lead to Replied
- update a lead to Has Stock / No Stock / Bad Lead / Do Not Contact
- confirm updates persist and display properly
- confirm optional notes/feedback work

## Guardrail checks
- no Buyer Follow-Up Queue anywhere
- no resizable split panes anywhere
- no accusatory vendor safety language
