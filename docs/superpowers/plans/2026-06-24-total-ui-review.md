## Overview

After PR0 the app has a sound, app-wide design system — one Trio-azure accent, tightened density locks, line tokens, and named cell utilities — but only two surfaces (Search/Part Dossier and Resell) were authored close enough to the foundation to have partially adopted it, and even they predate the final tokens. Every audited page carries the same dominant debt: active/selected states, primary CTAs, and focus rings still resolve to `brand-500/600/700/800` instead of `accent-*`, so the app reads in two competing blues. The second systemic gap is bespoke markup re-implementing primitives — hand-rolled tables (`min-w-full divide-y` with inline `px-4 py-3` / `font-medium gray-500` headers), filter pills, status badges, and buttons — which simultaneously creates density-debt, line-debt, and the sub-11px readability-floor violations. On the philosophy axis the bones are strong (dense split-panels, institutional-memory signals, trust cues), but the Three-Second Rule fails repeatedly on "Where am I?" (most pages have no title/breadcrumb) and on "What needs my attention?" (no urgency treatment on deadline/attention columns). The work ahead is overwhelmingly conformance and mechanical migration, not restructuring — which means it sequences cleanly as cheap app-wide sweeps first, then a thin layer of per-page hierarchy/workflow polish.

## Risks
- **Density too aggressive on data-heavy grids:** moving Buy Plans/CRM/Proactive tables to the 5px/10px locks meaningfully tightens row height; verify dense real-PG data (long MPNs, multi-line vendor names) still reads and that money columns stay aligned (tabular-nums).
- **Mobile 44px touch targets:** tightening `px-4 py-3`→`p-3` and bumping `text-[10px]`→`text-[11px]` must not shrink tappable controls below 44px on the Sightings/Resell/Sales Hub mobile cards; check the per-vendor and quick-action buttons specifically.
- **Sticky-header / split-panel offsets:** Sales Hub, Sightings, Search, Resell, Materials rely on sticky two-tier search rails and pinned panel headers; density changes to `<main>`/header padding can shift sticky `top` offsets and cause content to hide under the rail — re-verify scroll behavior per page.
- **Legacy `border-gray-* !important` override block:** until fully drained it can win specificity battles; converting hairlines page-by-page may leave mixed states where some borders still resolve via `!important`. Track which pages are drained and avoid removing the block until the last page lands.
- **`.input` width/layout regressions:** replacing bespoke inputs with `.input` changes default `w-full`/padding; flex-bar inputs (Sightings `flex-1 min-w-[150px]`, Search `flex-1`) need width utilities re-appended or they will collapse/overflow.
- **htmx/Alpine reactivity on pill migration:** the hand-rolled lens/filter pills exist partly because `filter_pill` doesn't expose Alpine `:class` reactivity (Resell) and hx-get behavior must be preserved — extend the macro or keep the hand-roll-with-matched-classes rather than blindly swapping, or the toggle state breaks.
- **False-green CI on accent sweeps:** large mechanical token swaps touch many templates; confirm Tailwind `accent-*` classes actually compiled into the deployed CSS (safelist) and verify in a headless browser, since curl won't catch a missing-class regression.
