# Product Vision

## Core Goal
Help purchasing agents find strong vendor leads to contact by email or phone and verify stock.

## What the sourcing engine should do
- aggregate possible vendor leads from multiple sources
- preserve source attribution
- explain why each lead was shown
- show lead confidence separately from vendor safety/risk
- help buyers act quickly
- capture outcomes so the system can improve over time

## What good looks like
A buyer can open the sourcing UI for a part and immediately answer:
- who might have stock?
- why do we think that?
- how fresh is the signal?
- is this vendor risky or trustworthy?
- what should I do next?

## Design philosophy
- buyer usefulness over technical elegance
- fewer strong, explainable leads over many weak ones
- human judgment remains central
- preserve evidence and source attribution
- avoid noisy workflows that create little value
