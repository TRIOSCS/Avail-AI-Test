# Interaction and Visual Rules

## Badge Language

### Confidence
- High Confidence
- Medium Confidence
- Low Confidence

### Safety
- Low Risk
- Medium Risk
- High Risk
- Unknown

## Default Lead Card Hierarchy
1. vendor name
2. confidence + safety
3. reason summary
4. source/freshness
5. contact preview
6. buyer status + next action
7. caution flags

## Visual Meaning
- confidence should feel like opportunity quality
- safety should feel like trust/caution
- risk flags should be noticeable but not overwhelming
- suggested action should read like guidance, not command

## Timeline / History
Keep simple:
- status changed
- note added
- feedback recorded
- lead created

## Microcopy examples

Good:
- "Recent marketplace signal with direct contact info."
- "Known vendor, but current stock signal is indirect."
- "Verify identity before contacting."
- "Limited business footprint; proceed with caution."

Avoid:
- "Definitely fraudulent"
- "Scammer"
- "Guaranteed stock"
